Who We Are

WARFIRE [WAR] is not just an alliance — it's a brotherhood of warriors who have earned their place at the top of Kingdom 1646. We are a Top 3 server alliance, built on discipline, coordination, and an unrelenting drive to dominate every event on the battlefield.

We believe in fair play and diplomacy — our NAP (Non-Aggression Pact) agreements are sacred, and we honor every treaty. Off the battlefield, we work together. On it, we are unstoppable.

We hold our events at community-voted times, because we win together — not alone. If you're ready to commit, WARFIRE is ready to fight alongside you.
